WELCOME TO OUR WEBSITE

For many years, the English Bulldog has been named the country's most
popular breed according to the Governing Council of the Dog Fancy (GCCF),
and with its affectionate nature and teddy-bear good looks, it's not hard
to see why. As far as personality goes, the English Bulldog is an amazing Dog.


ENGLISH BULLDOG BREED PROFILE

AVERAGE LIFESPAN..............................40


TEMPERAMENT......................................15 to 20 years


GROOMING............................... Friendly and affectionate, loyal and devoted, good with children and other pets.


WEIGHT................... Males 6.8 to 8.1kg; females 4.5 to 5.4kg


AVAILABILITY.............Easy


COLORS...................Available in almost every colour and coat pattern you can think of.


BREED NUMBER..............Once a week is adequate. A simple hand groom will often remove dead hair.

CHOOSING A DOG

Choosing between a male and a female is not an easy task, as both make
wonderful companions. Differences in personality are mostly attributable to
the fact that everyone has something unique about them, rather than to
male vs. female differences.
